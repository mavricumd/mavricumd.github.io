About us

MAVRIC launched in 2017 to promote and support Mixed, Augmented, and Virtual Reality creators, innovators, and users. By working with corporations, government agencies, startups, and researchers, MAVRIC has been supporting companies and institutions make smart investments in these Immersive Technologies (aka XR).

01

The Future of Wargaming at the Connections
Wargaming Conference

Held annually since 1993, the mission of Connections is to advance and preserve the art, science, and application of wargaming. The conference works each year to facilitate a useful exchange information on achievements, best practices and needs of all elements of the field of wargaming, from military, to commercial, to academic applications.
